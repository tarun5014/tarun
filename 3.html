To protect your mental health:

1. Prioritize self-care, including adequate sleep, exercise, and nutrition.
2. Maintain a support system of friends and family.
3. Manage stress through relaxation techniques like mindfulness or meditation.
4. Seek professional help when needed, without stigma.
5. Set realistic goals and boundaries to avoid overwhelm.
6. Monitor and address negative thought patterns.
7. Engage in activities you enjoy and find purpose in.
